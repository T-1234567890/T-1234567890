# Hello World

This is the first post on this site.

I didn’t want the first article to be anything special — just something simple and honest. Most side projects don’t begin with big ideas. They start with small experiments and unfinished thoughts.

Recently I’ve been spending more time building things slowly instead of trying to ship something perfect. I noticed that when I focus on keeping interfaces calm and simple, I enjoy the process more.

A few things I’m learning:

- Small progress is still progress.
- Clean design is mostly about removing noise (and deleting things).
- Shipping something small is better than planning forever.

Nothing fancy. Just notes and blog.

See you in the next post.
